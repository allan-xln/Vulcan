# Sales Demo

## Positioning

Vulcan is a central de inteligencia operacional. The demo must show where the company loses time, where it loses money, which agents are healthy, which teams are overloaded and what the manager should do next.

Do not sell it as employee surveillance. The message is:

`Vulcan mede fluxo operacional, nao conteudo pessoal.`

## 60 Second Story

1. Open the dashboard and point to the executive strip: perda financeira prioritaria, acao recomendada and prontidao do piloto.
2. Show online agents, events today, AI, WhatsApp and e-mail status.
3. Show `Onde a empresa esta perdendo dinheiro`.
4. Show `Acoes recomendadas pela IA`.
5. Open `Hierarquia` and login as a lower profile to prove scoped visibility.
6. Open `Metricas` and explain context switch, idle time and focus score in plain business language.
7. Open `Notificacoes` and show that alerts can leave the system by WhatsApp/e-mail.
8. Open `Configuracoes` and show that WhatsApp, e-mail, AI, Supabase, agents and privacy are configurable.

## Discovery Questions

- Onde sua equipe perde mais tempo hoje?
- Quanto custa uma hora improdutiva no seu setor?
- Quem precisa receber alerta sem entrar no painel?
- Voce quer acompanhar equipes remotas, presenciais ou ambas?
- Quais sistemas mais travam a operacao?
- Qual processo repetitivo deveria virar automacao primeiro?

## Value Narrative

- Productivity: active time, idle time and focus score make invisible waste visible.
- Management: hierarchy shows exactly what each level can see.
- Automation: insights rank repetitive processes and expected ROI.
- Reliability: agent health shows online/offline, queue, sync and collection quality.
- Action: AI recommendations become alerts, reports and operational plans.

## Close For Paid Pilot

Proposed pilot:

- 1 tenant.
- 1 to 3 departments.
- 10 to 30 agents.
- 14 to 30 days of measurement.
- Weekly executive report.
- WhatsApp/e-mail alerting for managers.
- Final ROI review with automation roadmap.

Exit criteria:

- Agents connected.
- Hierarchy validated.
- At least one executive report delivered.
- At least three bottlenecks identified.
- At least one automation opportunity with estimated ROI.
